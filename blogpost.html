10 reasons to apply to Le Wagon Chengdu

Or to any other cities of Le Wagon.

1st coding bootcamp in the world

Le Wagon is ranked first coding bootcamp in the world by the reference website Switchup.org. Le Wagon is a very product-driven bootcamp teaching Ruby, Ruby on Rails, JavaScript, HTML and CSS, APIs, Github, Heroku (among other things). The program brings coding skill to creative entrepreneurs to make them able to build their own application, find a job in the tech industry (as junior developer or product manager) and collaborate efficiently with their future dev team.

Coolest city ever!

Chengdu has one of the best performing regional economies in China, yet it maintains its rich cultural heritage and famous laid-back lifestyle. Throughout a decade of strong growth in the tech sector it has attracted companies such as IBM, Thoughtworks, Samsung, Tencent and over half of the Fortune 500 companies.
